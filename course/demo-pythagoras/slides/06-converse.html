Going the other way

The converse and triples

  • If a² + b² = c², the triangle must be right-angled
  • Whole-number side sets that fit are Pythagorean triples
  • Examples: 3-4-5, 5-12-13, 8-15-17
Handy Builders use a 3-4-5 measurement to check that a corner is truly square.
